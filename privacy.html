Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026. Geometry Dash Avenue is a static community website for server rules, staff information, and Geometry Dash level ideas. The site is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

Who This Covers

This policy covers the Geometry Dash Avenue website pages: Rules, Idea Generator, About Us, and this Privacy Policy. The Discord server itself runs on Discord, so activity inside Discord is also covered by Discord's own terms and privacy policy.

What This Website Collects

This website does not have accounts, sign-up forms, comments, payments, newsletters, analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or its own cookies. It also does not use localStorage or sessionStorage to save your generator choices.

Like most websites, the hosting service may automatically receive basic technical information when pages are loaded, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, requested page, and visit time. This is used for delivery, security, debugging, and abuse prevention.

Idea Generator

The Idea Generator runs in your browser. Random mode creates prompts locally. Personalized mode loads the public levels.json file and processes your choices in the browser to suggest a style direction.

Your generator choices and results are not sent to Geometry Dash Avenue, are not stored by this website, and should not include personal information.

External Services

Some parts of the site use third-party services:

  • The Join Us link opens Discord.
  • The About Us page loads staff profile images from Discord image servers.
  • The Idea Generator loads level preview images from ImgBB image servers.

When your browser opens Discord or loads images from Discord or ImgBB, those services may receive technical information from your device and may process it under their own policies. You can review Discord's policy at discord.com/privacy and ImgBB's policy at imgbb.com/privacy.

Discord Server Activity

If you join the Geometry Dash Avenue Discord server, the information you share there may include your Discord username, profile details, messages, attachments, voice activity, reports, moderation history, and any information you choose to post or send to staff.

Staff may use server information to keep the community safe, enforce the rules, respond to reports, investigate abuse, and handle appeals. Do not share private information about yourself or anyone else unless it is necessary and safe to do so.

Sharing And Retention

Geometry Dash Avenue does not sell personal information. Website data is limited to technical hosting logs controlled by the host. Discord data stays within Discord unless it must be shared with appropriate staff, Discord Trust and Safety, server partners for safety reasons, or legal authorities when required.

Because this website does not run its own user database, it does not keep website account records. Discord messages, moderation records, and safety evidence may be kept as long as needed for server safety, rule enforcement, appeals, or legal compliance.

Your Choices And Rights

You can use the website without joining Discord. You can also block third-party images in your browser if you do not want Discord or ImgBB images to load.

If privacy laws such as the GDPR apply to you, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of personal information connected to you. For Discord account data, use Discord's privacy controls or contact Discord. For Geometry Dash Avenue server moderation records or website questions, contact Geometry Dash Avenue staff through the Discord server.

Minors

This website is not designed to collect personal information from children. Many Geometry Dash players are young, so the safest rule is simple: do not post your real name, address, school, phone number, face, private accounts, or other identifying information on the website or in the Discord server.

Changes

If Geometry Dash Avenue adds forms, accounts, cookies, analytics, payments, or other features that collect more information, this policy should be updated before those features go live. Any important changes should be reflected on this page with a new update date.
